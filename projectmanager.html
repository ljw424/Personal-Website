I am a project manager.

My Skills as a Project Manager

  • My project begins every September with my target: I must ensure that my new team of 30 young people achieve age-related expectations within an 11 month time frame.
  • I produce a project plan detailing how and when each of the curriculum objectives will be taught and make contingency plans for children who may need extra support. I consider resourcing and budgeting implications.
  • I take into account the individual needs of my team members and adjust targets and ways of working accordingly.
  • I conduct continuous informal monitoring of progress towards targets and regular formal monitoring each half term. I involve all stakeholders in the monitoring process.
  • I effectively lead support staff, communicating with them the goals of the project and their role in achieving the goals. I listen to their feedback and, when appropriate, make adjustments to the project plan.
  • I approach the project flexibly recognising that changes will be necessary based on research and data. I implement these changes in a timely manner with the aim of meeting project deadlines.
  • I evaluate the project building upon strengths and acting upon areas for development in the next cycle.
